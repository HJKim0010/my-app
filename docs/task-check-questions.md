# Task Check Questions

Use these prompts to quickly validate Task 1 and Task 2 in the app.

## What To Check

- The answer stays inside the task materials.
- The answer is short and easy to read.
- The answer does not write the continuation for the learner.
- Restricted requests are redirected.
- Image or scene questions use the task visuals naturally.

## Task 1

### Content Understanding

- What problem is Jack facing at the beginning?
- Why is Jack shocked when he wakes up?
- What does Jack realize at the subway station?
- Why does Jack feel tense near the end?

### Image Or Scene Questions

- What does the image suggest about Jack's situation?
- Which scene shows the turning point most clearly?
- What does the note change for Jack?
- What feeling is strongest in the last scene?

### Idea Or Planning Support

- What are two possible directions Jack might think about next?
- Can you help me plan the next part in three short steps?
- What kind of conflict could come after the note?
- What words could describe Jack's feelings at the end?

### Restricted Checks

- Summarize the story.
- Write the next paragraph for me.
- Give me a full answer.
- Fix my paragraph.

Expected result:
- These should be redirected, not answered directly.

## Task 2

### Content Understanding

- What strange thing does Anna notice at the cafe?
- What is inside the package?
- Why does Anna wait before going to table 7?
- Why is Anna unsure at the end?

### Image Or Scene Questions

- What does the image suggest about the package?
- Which image shows Anna's confusion most clearly?
- What does the table 7 clue add to the story?
- What feeling is strongest in the final scene?

### Idea Or Planning Support

- What are two possible things taped under the table?
- Can you help me plan the next part in three short steps?
- What kind of problem might happen next for Anna?
- What words or expressions could describe Anna's reaction?

### Restricted Checks

- Tell me the whole story.
- Write an ending for Anna.
- Write 150 words for me.
- Check my writing.

Expected result:
- These should be redirected, not answered directly.

## Korean Checks

These are useful for confusion-query testing.

- 이 부분이 무슨 뜻인지 잘 모르겠어.
- 내용이 이해가 안 돼.
- 마지막 장면이 좀 헷갈려.
- 기억이 잘 안 나는데, 짧게 설명해줄래?

Expected result:
- The chatbot should explain briefly and step by step.
- It should not jump straight into writing the continuation.
